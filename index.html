Why I became a web developer

Well this is a long story.

I began my journey with a bachelor degree in cultural science. While I was studying I were fascinated by how to work with communication to gain trust and attention from a choosen target group and that´s why I choose to build (with help from a friend) a website to a choir in Gothenburg. I liked both the programming and communication part so after a short brief in my life I decided to continue my journey towards communication studies.

While I were studying communication I took every opportunity to build websites (again with help from a friend) and started to learn basic programming at my spare time. I also noticed I were more interested in communication on websites than write strategies.

So, eventually I tried my luck and applied to YRGOs web developer program and I were accepted. It´s been a hard and fun journey. The more I learn, the more I understand that there is so much more to learn. It can be a struggle but it is also fun to actually see my own progress. To look back at a code I wrote months ago and see how to improve it, make it more effective and more readable.

That is why I continue my journey. To get better, to learn new smarter ways, learn new programming languages and make better and safer websites.
